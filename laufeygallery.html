Who is Laufey


Laufey (pronounced lay-vay) is a 21st-century Icelandic singer-songwriter, cellist, and pianist whose music bridges the gap between past and present. Raised between Reykjavík and Washington, D.C., and deeply influenced by her classical violinist mother, she grew up listening to jazz standards alongside classical concertos.Today, she seamlessly blends the timeless, cinematic sweeping arrangements of traditional jazz with the relatable, intimate songwriting of modern bedroom pop.

Describing her style as "modern jazz," Laufey’s core mission is to bring the rich sounds of jazz and classical music back to her own generation. Through her velvety, old-soul vocals and masterful instrumental arrangements, she creates a cozy, nostalgic world that feels both entirely fresh and beautifully timeless.
